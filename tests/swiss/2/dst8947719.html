Level 2, moderate

Wet avalanches as day progresses

As a consequence of warming during the day and solar radiation more frequent medium-sized and, in isolated cases, large wet and gliding avalanches are to be expected. The avalanche prone locations for gliding avalanches are to be found in particular on steep sunny slopes below approximately 2400 m. Especially on very steep south facing slopes more wet loose snow avalanches are to be expected below approximately 3000 m. Additionally in some places wet avalanches can also release the entire snowpack and reach quite a large size. Backcountry tours should be concluded timely.
